The objective of this study is to understand the current awareness of personal wellbeing in gig-workers with a special focus on rideshare drivers. This study also aims to observe the effect of social sensing in promoting awareness of wellbeing in gig-workers.

BugSwarm
PI: Cindy Rubio Gonzalez

BugSwarm is a toolset that enables the creation of scalable, diverse, realistic, continuously growing set of reproducible build failures and fixes from open-source real-world systems. The BugSwarm dataset is the largest dataset of reproducible build fail-pass pairs and my work upto now has primarily been regarding visualization of this dataset and the user experience of the BugSwarm website.
